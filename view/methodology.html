We understand that

An effective transformation must address the way the IT function is organised and how it interfaces with its clients. We’ve got starting point operating models for key journeys including the projects needed to migrate to them

We want you to be in your business value chain. Our Transformation Partners will help you stay proactively aligned to your business. Use of dashboards tracking operational and transformation impact will help you do that. Our modular contracting approach together with our standard services make it easy to adapt to changing priorities.

In an agile world, a CAPEX-based approach to funding IT makes little sense. In that agile world a fixed contract is a brake on change. We’ll bring OPEX-based consumption models and a modular contracting approach all designed to keep you tracking your business

Our human capital management experience and our management of change framework, together with our broad training capability will help ensure that your workforce is able to operate in the transformed world

We’ll bring our service management experience together with our views on the right operating model for your needs and work with you to link those to the delivery of key business services. Your IT processes will better support your business outcomes

Our advisory-led approach brings the full breadth of HPE’s portfolio to support your journey. Our evergreening approach will ensure your infrastructure stays up to date.
