Multinational Consumer Goods Company

Business Need

HPE implemented AppSystems on SAP HANA for Clients business intelligence environment

The client relies heavily on business intelligence (BI) and wanted to grow topline revenues by leveraging all of the company's data for suppliers, customers, and more. The Client wanted to:

  • Produce reports/analytics to drive growth.
  • Redefine the ways the company harvests data.
  • Initiate real-time reporting.

HPE Solution

  • HPE has supported the client for many years and is aligned to their business direction.
  • Supported the client through the business case and decision-making process to identify their transformation journey.
  • Assessed the architectural impact through assessment of the current and future environments.
  • Proposed a roadmap detailing a phased hardware approach that included swapping out hardware after one year to provide the latest next-generation functionality and computing power.

Outcome

  • Better decisions and business outcomes, including increased productivity.
  • Better, faster, and cheaper performance of existing modules that the support business.
  • Opportunity to add functionality in the future to address the changing needs of their business.
