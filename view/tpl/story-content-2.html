Regional Government Agency

Business Need

HPE Services developed an IT transformation roadmap to support the initial IT set-up of the new agency.

  • The Agency is a joint initiative of the Clients Government and the Regional Council Community Commission. Its aim was to implement a joint information and communication policy and become a meeting place where organizations, associations and institutions become more visible.
  • The agency officially started with a charter to take over the role of two separate agencies.
  • The government body responsible for setting up the agency, wanted to investigate IT needed to run this new agency.

HPE Solution

  • Aligned on business direction through interview and elicitation.
  • Provided business transformation consulting to align IT functions to the business.
  • Performed an architectural assessment which allowed standard needs to be fulfilled with available generic solutions and specific needs with specialized solutions.
  • Drove management of change through alignment with stakeholders by visually representing the IT building blocks of the enterprise architecture.
  • Generated a full multi-year transformation roadmap that aligns with expected business direction.

Outcome

  • The Client used the proposed IT cost and plan to define Agency IT budget.
  • Agency management used and customized the transformation roadmap to support a successful launch from its new location.
  • The agency has now considered the initial roadmap finalized and entered a continuous optimization phase.
  • HPE continues to partner with the client.
