Automotive Manufacturer

Business Need

HPE plans and executes a successful migration to SAP HANA

  • Increase production output to 3 million vehicles by 2020
  • Improve response speed of SAP BW system
  • Reduce amount of system data and enhance overall user experience

HPE Solution

  • HPE reviewed the business requirements to ensure business direction alignment and Identify the clients transformation journey.
  • Conducted an assessment of the existing systems to help Scope required IS change and assess architectural impact.
  • Defined a transformation roadmap including.
  • Replacement of the existing Oracle database with SAP HANA in-memory computing platform for the SAP BW system.
  • Proposal of the SAP HANA Scale-Out System with multi-HANA nodes to balance the workload.

Outcome

  • Client experienced significant performance improvement, including faster report generation and response times.
  • Better and faster understanding of Clients business KPIs, improving management decision making.
  • Ability for Client to respond more quickly to customer feedback, improving customer satisfaction.
  • Scalable platform for growth.
