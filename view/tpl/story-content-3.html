Business Need

An HPE transformation consulting engagement resulted in a new Enterprise Architecture and transformation plan.

  • The Clients business strategy was moving towards a new generation of payment collection. However, it was impossible to expand their market and product offerings with their current IT architecture. The current technology did not allow this growth and was even at some risk in its ability to sustain the current operations.

HPE Solution

  • Aligned on business direction through assessment of business needs and current IT architecture.
  • Performed an architectural assessment through interview of technical stakeholders and hosting of workshops to understand the Information, infrastructure and security.
  • Drove Management of Change through generation of charts and diagrams showing business value and context , SWOT analysis, and a matrix of objectives and critical success factors.
  • Defined a new target enterprise architecture delivering a Framework that allows the Client to adopt this new architecture in phases, with a clear link between IT and business drivers.

Outcome

  • The Client now has a target Enterprise Architecture and an actionable plan to move from their current state to future state.
  • Future state has specific alignment to the business strategy and proper architectural decisions based on business needs.
